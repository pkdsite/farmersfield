About us

Freshness Guaranteed

FarmersField's strength is in delivering fresh and high-quality product. We enable the supply and delivery of freshest milk within a few hours of collection from the farm. For fruits and vegetables, a delivery from farm to your home is enabled within 10-hours time span thus assuring high quality and freshness, We believe that it is not only hygenic but also tasty to consume fresh products and this is a practice we work rigorously follow.

Transparency through Traceability

FarmersField enables transparency through traceability as it allows customers to know the source of the location of the product they purchase. A customer thus purchasing any fruit or vegetable will be able to know the location where it was produced and also the farmer who produced it. This further assures high-quality of the product and ensures the customer of the FarmersField brand assurance.

Convenient Subscription Model & Hassle-free Delivery

FarmersField offers a simple subscription model to the customer thus assuring great convenience. The customer can order in the 12-hour window from morning 9 am to evening at 9 pm. The order delivery is made next day morning between 7 am and 8 am. This offers custumer a great convenience to order and assures a hassle-free ordering and delivering experience. Customer satisfaction and timely order fulfilment is a promise we always keep.
